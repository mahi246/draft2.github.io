Real Estate

When many think of real estate, they think of buying vacation homes, plots of land etc.
However, real estate investing has much more to it. As a beginner, it is important to
understand the different aspects of investing in real estate, and also understanding
what is best for you and your portfolio.

Types of Real Estate Investments

  • Rental Properties
    •  
  • "Flipping" Homes
    •  
  • REIGs
    •  
  • REITs

Rental Properties

Rental properties are a good way to start investing in real estate. To begin, you will
need to start off by buying a property and renovate it if necessary. Thus, having a
rental property means you will need to start with a hefty amount of money to cover
maintenance and times your property will be vacant. However, if marketed well, landlords
can receive a regular stream of income. When renting out property it is important to
cover everything in the contract that will protect your property from damage done by renters.

"Flipping" homes

Flipping homes may not be for everyone because it is important to have knowledge on
valuation of homes and renovation. Likewise, you would need marketing knowledge and
time to oversee renovations. Just like rental properties, flipping homes requires a
substantial amount of money to buy and renovate the home. Profit occurs when you sell
the home for more than the amount you bought it, plus the cost to renovate. When
flipping a home, the housing marketplays a big role in the profit you will make.

Real Estate Investment Groups(REIGs)

REIGs are similar to mutual funds, but for real estate. Typically, a company would buy or
build, for example, a building and then investors would buy parts of it. The investors then
own parts of the building,however,it would be managed by the company that built or bought
the building.The company would help advertise and interview tenants for the building.

Real Estate Investment Trusts(REITs)

REITs help investors make an income from more commercial real estate properties.
Examples of such properties include hotels, apartment buildings and shopping malls.
REITs companies tend to develop properties specifically to add to their investment
portfolios. Quite a few REITs can be purchased through major stock exchanges.They
can also be purchased through a broker. The REITs companies give Investors profit
from the income produced from the real estate, based on how many shares they have.
