Bonds

What are bonds?

A bond is a debt security. When a bond is issued to you, it means you are lending
money to an institutions or company. You are lending them money till a certain date.
Therefore, you have to hold a bond until a certain period of time for it to “mature”.
Until the date issued, you will be paid interest and on the date you will receive the
amount you first issued. There are a few types of bonds: treasury, municipal and corporate.

Investing using bonds

Bonds can be bought through a broker. However, the US treasury bonds are sold by the
federal government. US treasury bonds can be bought from a bank or a broker with a fee.
Bonds can also be bought through mutual funds. This allows an investor to diversify their
investments. Bond funds also give monthly dividends and are liquid, meaning you can
buy and sell bond funds anyday.
